Hilary's Quality Cuisine

Family based professional caterers in Huddersfield since 1996

Welcome to Hilary's Quality Cuisine

We are friendly and professional family-based caterers with over 25 years of experience in high quality food.

Hilary's Quality Cuisine has provided high quality catering and support services at hundreds of different venues throughout the North of England.

We're proud to be an approved caterer to Kirklees Metropolitan Council and of our 5-Star Excellent grading in the national food hygiene rating system. We also hold the Kirklees Gold Award for healthy eating.

Choose from our buffet menus, or ask us about barbecue and custom menus.

We cater easily for any party size from 10 to 500+ guests

We can work in most venues or help you with marquee or venue hire.

We cater a wide range of events including weddings, commercial, private and coporate events
